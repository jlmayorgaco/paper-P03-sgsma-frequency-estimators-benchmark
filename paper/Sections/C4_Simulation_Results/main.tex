\subsection{Performance on Standard IEC/IEEE Scenarios}
% ---------------------------------------------------------

Table~\ref{tab:ieee_results} summarizes performance on standard scenarios (3 s.f.).
\textbf{Steady-state:} SRF-PLL achieves RMSE\,$=1.30\!\times\!10^{-4}$\,Hz (Modulation);
window-based methods reach RMSE\,$<3\!\times\!10^{-4}$\,Hz.
\textbf{Ramp:} The Ramp test exposes structural limitations. SOGI-FLL fails to track (RMSE\,$=1.12$\,Hz; type-1 loop). The RA-EKF achieves RMSE\,$=\mathbf{0.00823}$\,Hz---$8.15\!\times$ lower than SRF-PLL and $1.41\!\times$ lower than EKF.

\subsection{Robustness Under Severe IBR Disturbances}
% ---------------------------------------------------------

\subsubsection{Composite Islanding (Scenario D)}

Window-based methods exhibit spectral leakage: IpDFT reaches RMSE\,$=0.484$\,Hz (peak $2.91$\,Hz); TFT peak $2.74$\,Hz.
EKF/UKF, lacking phase-reset mechanisms, incur $T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0.122$\,s; SRF-PLL avoids leakage (RMSE\,$=0.0450$\,Hz, $T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0$).
The \textbf{RA-EKF} achieves RMSE\,$=\mathbf{0.0393}$\,Hz---a $\mathbf{5.7\!\times}$ RMSE reduction vs.\ EKF---with zero threshold breaches ($T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0$; peak $0.365$\,Hz $<0.5$\,Hz, Fig.~\ref{fig:dashboard_mega}g).

\subsubsection{Multi-Event Stress Test (Scenario E)}

Table~\ref{tab:risk_metrics} quantifies trip-risk under the IBR Multi-Event sequence (5th/7th harmonics; THD\,$\approx$\,4.5\%, IEEE~519 compliant). SOGI-FLL incurs the highest trip-risk ($3.10$\,s); SRF-PLL accumulates $T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0.353$\,s (RMSE\,$=0.307$\,Hz). The \textbf{RA-EKF} achieves RMSE\,$=0.159$\,Hz with $T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0.081$\,s at $88.5\,\mu$s/sample---a $\mathbf{4.4\!\times}$ trip-risk reduction vs.\ SRF-PLL and $\mathbf{4.5\!\times}$ vs.\ EKF (event-gating). IpDFT: $T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0.563$\,s; Koopman: $0.068$\,s.

\subsection{Computational Feasibility}
% ---------------------------------------------------------

Fig.~\ref{fig:dashboard_mega}e and Table~\ref{tab:complexity} show the
cost--risk Pareto frontier. \textbf{Low cost ($\leq$30\,$\mu$s):} SRF-PLL
($16.7\,\mu$s) and EKF ($29.5\,\mu$s) are the least expensive; SRF-PLL,
however, incurs elevated trip-risk under the composite Multi-Event
sequence ($T_{\mathrm{trip}}=0.353$\,s; Table~\ref{tab:risk_metrics}).
\textbf{Moderate (30--100\,$\mu$s):} SOGI-FLL ($41.7\,\mu$s), TFT
($45.6\,\mu$s), and RA-EKF ($88.5\,\mu$s) are plausible candidates for
embedded DSP/FPGA relay platforms, pending C or hardware-in-the-loop
validation. \textbf{Higher cost ($>$100\,$\mu$s):} IpDFT ($110\,\mu$s),
Koopman ($120\,\mu$s), UKF ($215\,\mu$s), and PI-GRU
(${\approx}154\,\text{ms/sample}$) are included for reference; PI-GRU
is prohibitive without hardware acceleration.
